Below are a list of food and hunger related non profits in the Milwakee wisconsisn area

Feeding America Eastern Wisconsin -

We are Feeding America Eastern Wisconsin – the largest hunger relief organization in the state of Wisconsin. Every meal we help provide is a step toward a stronger, healthier community. Working hand-in-hand with generous donors, suppliers, pantries, and compassionate volunteers, we deliver nutritious food and vital resources, turning hunger into hope for families, children, and seniors. Together, we’re creating a future where every plate is full, and every neighbor feels supported.

Milwaukee Hunger Task Force -

Mission Statement:

Hunger Task Force believes that every person has a right to healthy food obtained with dignity. We work to prevent hunger and malnutrition by providing food to people in need today and by promoting social policies to achieve a hunger free community tomorrow.

Core Values:

Our core values are dignity, justice, equity, compassion and stewardship. Our goal and mission is to end hunger in our community and state.

The Salvation Army Milwaukee County -

The Salvation Army has been meeting human needs without discrimination since 1865. From our iconic annual Red Kettle Campaign to our wide variety of available programs that serve local communities, we are dedicated to serving our neighbors in need.
